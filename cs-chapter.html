Message from HOD, CSE


The IEEE Computer Society student branch is positioned to create the technological leader of tomorrow. Our goal is to ensure that our engineering graduates are well prepared to play the roles of problem solvers, project leaders, entrepreneurs, innovators, data scientists and above all ethical citizens of a global society. Our aim is to provide professional grooming and guidance so that our students mould into thorough and inspiring individuals when they enter into the real-time environment.

- Prof. Vivek Kulkarni,
   HOD, CSE, Vardhaman College of Engineering.
